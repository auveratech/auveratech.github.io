The Auvera Process of Providing Solutions


Auvera technologies was founded with the vision of addressing the need for specific need for specialized automation efforts that often lacks within teams or workflow processes. At Auveratech, we take an in-house approach to problem-solving coupled with the agile methodology to provide fast and meaningful results from our first initial deep dive analysis.

Phase 1 Data Collection
During Phase 1 we conduct the initial deep dive to collect data, facts, or logical points of interest in a given workflow. Data is collected for mapping and architecting dynamic solutions.

Phase 2 Refine Information
For Phase 2 we refine the data collected into measurable and quantifiable information that also becomes imperative for constructing meaningful countermeasures, alternatives, or tangents.

Phase 3 Provide Intelligence
The final phase, we engineer actionable intelligence for your workflow in the form of process automation, performance indicators, statistical analysis and more.
